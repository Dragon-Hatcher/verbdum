HOW TO PLAY

Guess the VERBDUM in 6 tries or fewer.

Each guess must be a five letter Latin word.

After each guess the letters will be colored based on how close they were to the correct word.

Examples

C A N I S

The letter A is in the word and in the right place.

L I T U S

The letter T is in the word but not in the right place.

H O R A E

The letter E is not in the word.

There is a new VERBDUM each day.

See acknowledgments.
